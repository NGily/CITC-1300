One Of My Favorite Geckos

Haveing over a thousand variants in a species makes it difficult to talk about EVERY gecko ever discoverd and classified. With that difficulty in mind I'm just going to share a few noteworthy favorites of mine. The first gecko I would like to talk about is Eublepharis macularius, or more commonly known as the "Leopard gecko." This is a popular lizard among pet owners for its distinct yellow body and black spots. Compared to other reptiles, lepard geckos are on the easier side for care, this is the primary reason for their popularity along with appearances.

Fun Facts

  • Geckos can regrow their tails.
  • They can climb on almost anything.
  • Some species can live from up to 10-20 years.
  • Geckos can make a wide variety of sounds.
